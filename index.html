Privacy Policy

We believe your data is yours alone.

Last updated: April 12, 2026

ExchangeCalSync ("the App") is a macOS menu-bar application that synchronizes calendar events from a Microsoft Exchange Server into Apple Calendar. Your privacy is paramount. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

ExchangeCalSync does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or any third party.

All data processed by the App stays entirely on your local device.

Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data locally on your Mac only:

  • Exchange username & password — Stored securely in the encrypted macOS Keychain, used exclusively to authenticate with your authorized Exchange server.
  • Sync state & mappings — Stored in a local SQLite database in Application Support to track incremental event updates efficiently.
  • Application logs — Stored locally for debugging purposes. Any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is automatically redacted before logging.

Network Communication

The App connects strictly to the specific Exchange sever URL you configure during setup. All underlying connections are forced over HTTPS (utilizing TLS 1.2+ protocols). The App never reaches out to external analytics services, telemetry dashboards, or tracking endpoints.

Calendar Access

The App requests explicit permission to access Apple Calendar via the native macOS EventKit framework solely to populate your synced schedule. Your local calendar data is inherently protected by macOS Sandboxing rules and is never transmitted out.

Data Retention

Your configuration remains on your device for the duration the App is installed. Uninstalling the application natively cleans up local database files. You may manually clear stored credentials at any time in the App's "Account" settings tab or via the Keychain Access utility.

Changes & Contact

If this policy structurally changes, the updated version will be reflected directly on this page with a new timestamp.

For questions or support regarding data handling, please refer to our documentation.
